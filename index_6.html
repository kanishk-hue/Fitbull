The Mission

India's Premier
Gym Equipment Manufacturer

Established as a Partnership firm in 1975, Premier Rubbers is a leading Manufacturer, Wholesaler, Exporter, Supplier and Importer of a wide range of gym machines and equipment — operating under the FitBulls brand.

We are a self-reliant company — from raw material (casting used in plates and dumbbells) to the finished product, everything is manufactured in-house across our four production units. Automated machinery for rubber processing, kneaders, mixers, cutting, welding and moulding ensures consistent, premium quality every time.

Under the headship of Mr. Samyak Jain, we have secured a respectable position in the fitness equipment domain — supplying gyms, athletes, wholesalers and exporters across the nation and beyond.

Est. 1975
Our Journey

Milestones

1975

Founded in Meerut

Premier Rubbers established as a Partnership firm in Meerut, Uttar Pradesh — the heart of India's sports goods manufacturing hub. Started with rubber gym equipment.

1990s

Scaling Production

Expanded to multiple manufacturing units with automated machinery — rubber processing kneaders, mixers, cutting, welding and moulding machinery installed for consistent quality.

2000s

Export & Wholesale Growth

Became a recognized Wholesaler, Exporter and Supplier of gym equipment across India and internationally. GST registered: 09AABFP0071D1ZP.

2010s

FitBulls Brand Launch

Launched the FitBulls brand — covering Bouncer Dumbbells, Hex Rubber Dumbbells, Bumper Plates, Captain America Plates and the full Phantom / TE / FLD commercial machine series.

Today

4 Units, 500+ Products, Pan-India

Four manufacturing units, 500+ SKUs, and a growing network of gym owners, distributors and exporters across India. Led by Mr. Samyak Jain, Premier Rubbers continues to set the benchmark for made-in-India gym equipment.
